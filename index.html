About John

From blueprints to backyards.

John MacEwen didn't start his career in real estate — he started it as an engineer, trained to break big problems into clear, solvable parts. That training never left him. It just found a new application.

"A home purchase is the most important project most people ever manage. I treat it like one."

For over 12 years, John has guided buyers and sellers across Toronto and the GTA through every stage of the process, bringing the same structured, detail-driven approach he once applied to engineering problems to negotiations, contracts, and market strategy. He's now a licensed real estate Broker, a designation that reflects both his experience and the added responsibility he's earned in the field.

Clients work with John because he's direct, organized, and genuinely invested in getting the details right — the same qualities that made him a good engineer make him a broker people trust with one of the biggest decisions of their lives.
